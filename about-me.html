My Home-State of Texas

Whataburger

The fast food chain restaurant, Whataburger, is perhaps the most famous one in Texas. Many know it for their spicy ketchup, "A" line designs, and yummy food. I worked at Whataburger for 5 years while living in Texas and I still love the food, that should tell you something!

Kolaches in Texas

I didn't realize that kolaches were a "texas thing" until I moved away when I joined the military and I couldn't find them anywhere! I dug into the history of kolaches and why they're so popular in Texas. A fascinating aspect of texas is the multitude of cultures that have settled in the state and how those cultures have mixed with texan culture to, eventually over decades, create it's own distint culture unique to the area. Texas kolaches are one of the many example of this!

Dinosaur State Park

Dinosaur State Park is in Glenrose, Texas. It is a place where you can observe real life dinosaur foodprints left imprinted in the stone. I went camping every year as a kid for my birthday, because I loved it so much. As an adult, any time I get the chance to visit my home state, I make sure to stop by and hike.

Places I've Lived

Washington State

I lived in Washington state for 5 years. I was stationed in Oak Harbor, Washington for 3 of those years and I stayed for 2 years after I got out of the military. I lived in Everett, Washington for the majority of my time living in the state. One of the apartments I lived in overlooked the ocean.


Coronado, SD, California

I lived on Coronado Island in San Diego, California when I was in "C" school in the Navy. I was there for a little over 3 months. When it was time to graduate and fulfill my orders to Washington state, I didn't want to go! I loved my time there including all of the delicious food, the beaches, and the weather.


Portland, Oregon

I moved from Washington state to Oregon in 2021. I moved around in airbnb's for a few months before finding an area and an apartment I wanted to live in. I lived in Portland, Oregon for a year before moving to the current area I am living in now. My time in Oregon was a mixed bag of experiences. I did not like the city but the nature spots of Oregon are gorgeous.
