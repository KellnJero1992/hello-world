I love to learn languages!

With my experience in learning several human languages, I decided to embark on the new journey of learning computer languages. I am currently studying Mandarin and American Sign Language with plans on also studying Spanish and German.

My love for languages started as a child, thumbing through books at the local library on how to learn various languages. I dreamt of the day I would be old enough to take classes and learn as many languages as my brain could hold. A large part of language learning is learning about cultures, history, traditions, and learning to think how other people think who speak those languages natively. It gives an opportunity to see from others' perspectives.
